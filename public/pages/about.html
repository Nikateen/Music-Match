About



Many people listen to the same few songs on repeat

We dont like to explore new and varied music; Thus, people end up playing the same track over and over again.This continues until either the song is ruined for them and they cannot physically listen to it again or they push it to the back of a playlist never to be seen again. Our website offers a small solution to this problem by instantly providing you with samples of new songs based on a few criteria, helping you burst out of your "playlist bubble"
